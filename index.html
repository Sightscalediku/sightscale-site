Why AI needs structure

Probabilistic models are powerful but unreliable under constraints. Deterministic systems are reliable but inflexible.

Neuro-symbolic systems combine both—translating natural language intent into structure, learning where ambiguity exists, and enforcing rules where correctness matters.
